Flex Grow and Shrink

Made while following along with interactive guide to flexbox. Learning more about how flexbox works, especially flex-basis, flex-grow and flex-shrink.

A form that gives four layouts

Depending on the size of the page, the form wraps. The space given to email increases more than the other two elements as email addresses are usually longer. Set this up with flex: 2;

Using margin auto to assign empty space.
